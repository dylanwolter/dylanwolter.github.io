Passion for IT

Hi, I'm Dylan! When I was 8 years old, much to my parents’ dismay, I decided to take apart our new family computer to figure out what was inside and how it all worked. Even after running some of the dirty parts through our dishwasher to clean them up, I was able to re-assemble all the components and bring our Macintosh LC 520 back to life. I like to think that was officially when my passion for IT was born and I have never looked back.

About Me

Hi there, I’m Dylan. I like to help different types of organizations develop & deploy video-centric unified communication strategies. Over the last 12 years, I’ve implemented video conferencing infrastructure that has enabled thousands of users to enjoy high-quality video experiences at home and in the office.

As a lifelong learner, I’ve also continued to gain skills along the way as my career has progressed. I hold industry certifications from many different technology companies for both collaboration products and Audio-Visual design.

Presently, I am enrolled in the MIT xPro Full Stack Web Development Certificate program to enhance my programming skills as modern collaboration platforms like Zoom and WebEx have become increasingly dependent on the use of web languages and developer tools.
